# Market Sizing Report: Khóa học tiếng Anh giao tiếp cho người đi làm tại Việt Nam

**Date**: 2026-05-14
**Niche**: Online English communication course for Vietnamese white-collar professionals
**Course price (given)**: 2.500.000 VND/khóa (~USD 95)
**Profile**: Solo creator, no large ad budget (organic-only Year 1)
**Verdict**: PROCEED — but with realistic Year 1 revenue target of **180-275M VND**, not "scale to billions". See SOM section for the math.

---

## Executive Summary

Thị trường khóa học tiếng Anh giao tiếp cho người đi làm tại Việt Nam là một thị trường **lớn, đang tăng trưởng (+12.11% CAGR digital segment), nhưng đông đối thủ**. TAM ~12-15 nghìn tỷ VND, SAM ~900 tỷ VND (urban white-collar, có willingness-to-pay). SOM Year 1 cho solo creator chỉ organic là **180-275M VND** (60-110 students). Đây là target realistic, không yêu cầu market share > 0.03%. Niche khả thi nhưng phải differentiation rõ (micro-niche vertical) để không bị ELSA Speak, ZIM, Jaxtina, ILA nuốt.

## (c) SOM Year 1 — Realistic for Solo Creator, No Ads

### Constraints

1. Solo creator, no ad budget → relies on **organic** (YouTube, TikTok, FB content, SEO, referrals).
2. Year 1 = building audience from near-zero.
3. Vietnamese course-creator playbook: FB Group + free-content funnel + low-touch sales.

### Path A — Bottom-up (audience × conversion × price)

```
Realistic Year 1 organic reach (consistent niche solo creator):
  YouTube + TikTok + FB cumulative reach by month 12 = 50,000-100,000

Warm audience capture (email list / FB group) at 2-3% of reach:
  = 1,500-3,000 engaged warm leads

Conversion warm → paid course:
  Industry benchmark: 1-10% (Teachfloor, Sellcoursesonline, LearnWorlds)
  Solo creator no-ads warm audience realistic: 3-5%
  Midpoint: 4%

Year 1 paying students = 1,500-3,000 × 4% = 60-120 students
Year 1 revenue         = 60-120 × 2.5M VND = 150M - 300M VND
```

### Path B — Top-down (market share sanity)

```
SAM Year 1                = 900 tỷ VND
Realistic share (new solo, no ads): 0.02-0.03%
SOM Year 1                = 900 tỷ × 0.025% = 225 million VND
```

### SOM Year 1 = **180-275M VND** (midpoint ~225M, ~90 students)

### Key Assumptions

- Creator publishes 2-3 pieces of content/week consistently for 12 months.
- Micro-niche differentiation exists (industry/role/outcome specificity).
- Sales funnel: free content → email/FB group → free webinar/mini-class → 2.5M course offer.
- No paid ads, no partnerships Year 1.
- Course completion + testimonial loop seeds Year 2 referral engine.

| Check | Threshold | Result | Pass? |
|---|---|---|---|
| SOM Year 1 < 100M VND → niche too small | Must be > 100M | 180-275M VND | PASS |

| Market growth direction | Stable or rising | +12.11% CAGR digital segment (IMARC) | PASS |
| Competition density | Manageable | High — ELSA (10M users), ZIM, Jaxtina, ILA, 51Talk, Prep, Talkfirst + dozens of solo creators | RISK — must micro-niche |

**Red flag**: Competitive density is high. Niche passes sizing but fails differentiation test unless creator picks a **specific vertical** (industry/role/use-case). Generic "tiếng Anh giao tiếp đi làm" will be drowned out by big centers.

## (e) Recommendation

### Decision: **PROCEED with micro-niche positioning**

### Rationale

1. **Market big enough**: SAM 900 tỷ VND comfortably supports a solo creator reaching 180-275M VND Year 1 without needing meaningful market share (< 0.03%).
2. **Demand structurally rising**: Decision 2371/QĐ-TTg (Oct 2025) makes English a second language priority 2025-2035; digital English learning CAGR 12.11%; 50% of learners motivated by career.
3. **Price point correct**: 2.5M VND sits in the sweet spot — above online commodity (1-2M) but below center-based offline (6.9-17M). Targets "I want quality but can't justify ILA" segment.
4. **Generic positioning will fail**: ELSA Speak + ZIM + Jaxtina + ILA dominate broad "tiếng Anh đi làm" search. Solo creator without ad budget cannot outspend.

### Required differentiation (pick ONE)

- **By industry**: "Tiếng Anh cho dân IT/Developer", "cho Sales B2B", "cho HR/Recruiter"
- **By outcome**: "Tiếng Anh để pass phỏng vấn FDI/MNC trong 90 ngày"
- **By format**: "Tiếng Anh giao tiếp 15 phút/ngày qua roleplay AI" (blue ocean vs traditional centers)
- **By persona**: "Cho người đi làm bận, đã thử nhiều khóa thất bại — phương pháp neuroscience-based"

### Year 1 Revenue Target

- **Realistic**: 200M VND (~80 students × 2.5M)
- **Stretch**: 275M VND (~110 students)
- **Pessimistic floor**: 100M VND (~40 students) — still passes "niche too small" gate

### Next steps (90 days)

1. **Week 1-2**: Pick micro-niche; validate via 10 audience interviews (FB Group polls + 1:1 calls).
2. **Week 3-8**: Content engine — 2 YouTube videos/week + 1 deep blog/week targeting niche long-tail keywords (e.g., "tiếng Anh phỏng vấn vị trí business analyst").
3. **Week 9-12**: Soft launch beta cohort 10-20 students at discounted 1.5M for testimonials + curriculum iteration.
4. **Month 4-12**: Scale content + monthly free webinars → email list 1,500-3,000 → quarterly cohorts at full 2.5M.

### Success metrics (12 months)

- Warm audience (email + FB group): 2,000+ engaged
- YouTube subs: 5,000+ (niche, not vanity)
- Paying students: 80-110
- Revenue: 200-275M VND
- Completion rate: > 60% (signal for Year 2 referral engine)
