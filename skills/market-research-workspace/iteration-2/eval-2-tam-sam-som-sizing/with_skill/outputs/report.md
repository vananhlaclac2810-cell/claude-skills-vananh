**Date:** 2026-05-14
**Niche:** English conversation course for working professionals (VN)
**Price point:** 2.5M VND / khóa
**Profile constraint:** Solo creator, không có ngân sách ads lớn
**Mode:** C — Sizing only (per `SKILL.md`)

## TL;DR

- **TAM** = ~25,125 tỷ VND (10.05M người service-sector career-motivated × 2.5M giá khóa).
- **SAM realistic cho solo @ tier 2.5M** = ~942 tỷ VND, ~377K addressable buyers.
- **SOM Year 1 solo no-ads** = **37.5M – 150M VND** (15–60 buyers), midpoint **~87.5M VND** — dưới sàn 100M VND.
- **Red flag chính:** SOM Y1 < 100M floor (midpoint) + TAM/SOM ratio 287,143x (cực fragmented) + competition cực dày (ELSA 5M users VN, Langmaster/Jaxtina/ZIM/VUS đã chiếm SEO + ads + offline).
- **Recommendation:** PIVOT — niche down hyper-narrow (English cho IT/HR/Sale B2B), HOẶC raise price + add coaching tier 6–10M, HOẶC accept Y1 = side income build proof for Y2 scale.

## 2. TAM — Total Addressable Market

### Formula
```
TAM = N_buyer × Avg_price
```

### N_buyer derivation
- VN employed Q2-2025 = 52.0M (NSO)
- Service sector share = 38.7% → ~20.1M người (proxy white-collar / customer-facing)
- % "career-motivated to improve English" (working-age adults aspiring promotion/better pay) ≈ 50%
  - Justification: IMARC digital English CAGR 12.11% confirms expanding paid interest; Vietnam Briefing classifies English as career premium; cross-check ELSA 5M VN users / 20M service-sector = 25% are already actively engaged digital learners → 50% career-motivated is reasonable upper proxy (50% includes both active and intent-only).

```
N_buyer = 20.1M × 50% = 10.05M người
```

### Avg_price
- **2.5M VND** (user-defined). Positioning check: 2.5M nằm giữa Unica self-paced 299K-800K (mass) và ZIM 4.95M / VUS 5.6M / Jaxtina 7M+ (premium offline-hybrid) — đúng zone "mid-online live group" tier.

### TAM calculation
```
TAM = 10.05M × 2,500,000 VND = 25,125,000,000,000 VND ≈ 25,125 tỷ VND
```

### Cross-validation (sanity)
- IMARC: VN digital English market 2024 = USD 38.45M ≈ 960 tỷ VND (current actual digital spend only).
- Statista: VN total online education 2025 = USD 397.5M ≈ 9,940 tỷ VND.
- TAM = 25,125 tỷ là "ceiling potential if all 10M motivated workers bought a 2.5M course"; ratio TAM-potential / current-digital-actual = ~26x, reasonable vì hầu hết working adults chưa take paid online English course → TAM = future-state ceiling, not current.

**→ TAM = ~25,125 tỷ VND**

## 3. SAM — Servable Addressable Market

Apply 4 filters (geo × demo × psycho × price tier fit) per `tam-sam-som-vn.md`.

### Filter 1 — Geographic: 50%
- **Rationale:** Solo digital creator chỉ effective serve audience đô thị có internet stable + thói quen học online. Top 4 metro (HCMC 9.57M + Hanoi 8.68M + Đà Nẵng + Bình Dương) ≈ 30% dân, nhưng concentrate ~50% of service-sector workers (urban skew vì service jobs tập trung đô thị).
- **Source:** MacroTrends HCMC; VietnamNet Hanoi 2024-25; World Bank urban 40.19%.

### Filter 2 — Demographic: 50%
- **Rationale:** Target age 25–40 (career-peak, promotion-driven English motivation cao nhất). Trong service-sector employed pool, age 25–40 ≈ 50% of working-age (Worldometer VN: 67.6% working-age population, half within 25-40 bracket).
- **Source:** Worldometer Vietnam demographics 2026.

### Filter 3 — Psychographic: 30%
- **Rationale:** "Digital-ready learner" — đã từng dùng app/online course, willing học từ remote creator. ELSA Speak 5M VN users = digital-native English learner proxy = 5M / 20M service workers ≈ 25%. Add ~5% non-ELSA digital learners (Duolingo, podcast, YouTube). → 30%.
- **Source:** VnExpress ELSA 5M VN; IMARC digital learning CAGR 12.11% confirms expanding psychographic pool.

### Filter 4 — Price tier fit: 50%
- **Rationale:** 2.5M VND = ~30% of avg monthly income 8.2M (NSO) → afford được nhưng cần motivation. Reference doc uses 50% for the same English tier. Pricing landscape:
  - <2.5M: Unica self-paced 299K-800K (mass market)
  - 2.5M-5M: Online live group, hybrid (target zone)
  - >5M: ZIM 4.95M, VUS 5.6M, Jaxtina 7M+ (premium offline/1-1)
- **Source:** Langmaster benchmark + HomeCredit competitor review + NSO income.

### SAM calculation
```
SAM = TAM × (geo × demo × psycho × price_fit)
    = 25,125 tỷ × 50% × 50% × 30% × 50%
    = 25,125 × 0.0375
    = ~942 tỷ VND

N_addressable = 10.05M × 0.0375 = ~377K người
```

### Cross-validate
- Reference doc Worked Example 2 gives SAM = 281 tỷ với 3M white-collar base. Scaling to our 10M service-sector base: 281 × (10/3) ≈ 937 tỷ — converges to ~942 tỷ ✓.
- SAM/TAM = 942/25,125 = **3.75%** (flag in Sanity Check section — borderline below 5%).

**→ SAM = ~942 tỷ VND | ~377K addressable buyers**

## 4. SOM Year 1 — Solo Creator, No Ads

### Constraints
- Solo (1 người: content + sale + delivery)
- Không ngân sách ads lớn (assume ≤2M VND/tháng test ads, không scale paid)
- Cold start: 0 brand vs. ELSA 5M VN users, Langmaster/Jaxtina/ZIM/VUS/British Council/Topica/Pasal đều established với ad budget + SEO + offline footprint
- Y1 = build audience + first launch cohort

### Bottom-up math

**Step 1 — Warm audience build over 12 tháng (organic):**
- Tháng 1-3: 0 → 1K FB/TikTok followers (slow niche-test)
- Tháng 4-9: 1K → 5K followers (1 viral hit + steady 2-3x/tuần upload)
- Tháng 10-12: 5K → 10K followers (compounding + small 1-2M ads test)
- Year-end: ~10K followers × 30% engaged English-intent = **~3K warm**
- Email list (lead magnet "100 mẫu câu English phỏng vấn"): ~500-1,000 subs
- Cross-check: Reference doc Worked Example 2 estimates 0→10K / 12 months, ~3K engaged → matches.

**Step 2 — Cold reach via organic content:**
- 30K-50K cold impressions/tháng cuối Y1 (after content thrived)
- Few % drip into warm pool over time → already reflected in 3K warm + 500-1K email

**Step 3 — Conversion warm → buyer:**
- LearnWorlds benchmark: 2-5% warm, 3.5% real example.
- English niche adjustment: -1% vì (a) competition cực dày — ELSA 5M users, Langmaster/Jaxtina/ZIM/VUS/British Council/Topica/Pasal đều established; (b) tier 2.5M không phải impulse buy → cold-start solo brand convert thấp hơn benchmark.
- **Realistic conversion range: 0.5% – 1.5%**

**Step 4 — Buyer count & revenue:**

| Scenario | Warm pool | Conv % | Buyers | Revenue (2.5M each) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Pessimistic | 3,000 | 0.5% | 15 | **37.5M VND** |
| Realistic (midpoint) | 3,500 | 1.0% | 35 | **87.5M VND** |
| Optimistic (with 1-2M ads test) | 4,000 | 1.5% | 60 | **150M VND** |

**→ SOM Y1 range: 37.5M – 150M VND | Midpoint: ~87.5M VND**

## 5. SANITY CHECK — 5 Red Flag Tests

Per `tam-sam-som-vn.md`:

| # | Test | Threshold | Our Number | Result |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **SOM Y1 < 100M VND floor** | <100M = niche quá nhỏ cho solo | Midpoint 87.5M; range 37.5M–150M | ⚠️ **TRIGGERED** (midpoint below floor) |
| 2 | **SOM > 1% market share** | >1% = over-optimistic | 87.5M / 942,000M SAM = **0.0093%** | ✓ PASS — conservative |
| 3 | **TAM/SOM ratio > 1000x** | >1000x = market fragmented, hard to capture | 25,125,000M / 87.5M = **287,143x** | ⚠️ **TRIGGERED** (extremely fragmented) |
| 4 | **SAM < 5% TAM** | <5% = filter quá aggressive OR niche không serviceable | 942/25,125 = **3.75%** | ⚠️ **BORDERLINE** (just under 5%) |
| 5 | **SAM > 30% TAM** | >30% = filter quá lỏng | 3.75% | ✓ PASS — not too loose |

**Score: 2 hard red flags (#1, #3) + 1 borderline (#4) = NICHE FAILS sanity check.**

### Diagnose
- Niche "English giao tiếp người đi làm" = MASS market với gigantic incumbents. ELSA Speak alone 5M VN users + Langmaster/VUS/Jaxtina/ZIM/Topica/British Council/Pasal đều có ad budget + SEO ranking + offline footprint.
- TAM/SOM 287,143x = market quá fragmented → solo creator capture micro-share.
- Floor 100M không đạt được ở midpoint → niche broad không đủ feed solo full-time Y1.
- SAM/TAM 3.75% < 5% threshold indicates filter aggressive (justified given competitive density) OR TAM denominator quá rộng cho 1 solo angle.

## 6. RECOMMENDATION & ACTION

### Verdict: **MARGINAL → PIVOT REQUIRED**

Niche "khóa học tiếng Anh giao tiếp cho người đi làm" @ 2.5M là **không khả thi cho solo no-ads Y1 ở dạng broad**. Cần 1 trong 3 paths:

### Path A — Niche down hyper-narrow (RECOMMENDED)
- **Action:** Pick sub-segment cực hẹp với pain rõ ràng:
  - "English cho IT engineer phỏng vấn job MNC / remote" — audience ~500K dev VN, willingness-to-pay cao (lương 2-3x sau khi nâng English)
  - "English cho HR/recruiter VN làm việc với client nước ngoài"
  - "English cho sale B2B công ty xuất khẩu"
- **Why:** Narrower = less competition (ELSA/Langmaster không serve niche này specific), pain rõ → conversion 2-3x cao hơn.
- **Expected SOM Y1:** 100M-250M VND (clears floor; cohort 40-100 buyers @ 2.5M)
- **First step:** Validate qua 10 customer discovery interview với 1 sub-segment cụ thể (use skill `vpd-customer-discovery-interview`).

### Path B — Raise price + add coaching tier
- **Action:** Pivot offer thành ladder: 2.5M self-paced + 8M group coaching 8-tuần + 18M 1-1 mentorship 3 tháng (Hormozi value ladder).
- **Math:** Y1 cần chỉ 15 buyers at 8M avg = 120M VND, clears floor.
- **Why:** Solo capacity-constrained → time premium > volume.
- **First step:** Re-design offer với skill `vpd-offer-packaging-alex-homozi`.

### Path C — Accept Y1 = side income, build for Y2
- **Action:** Treat 87.5M Y1 = ~7.3M/tháng = side income while keeping main job/freelance. Use Y1 để build content + 5-10 case study, Y2 scale với traction.
- **Risk:** Burnout (12 tháng cô đơn competing với well-funded incumbents).

### Hard rule trước khi launch
Nếu KHÔNG niche down + KHÔNG raise price + KHÔNG accept side-income framing → **PASS this niche**, quay lại Phase 1 (skill `market-research` Mode B) tìm niche khác có TAM/SOM <1000x.
